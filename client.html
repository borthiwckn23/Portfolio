The Pre-Health Club's Website

As part of my DCI-108: Communication Through the Web course at Washington and Lee University, three Washington and Lee University students and I built a website for our client; Washington and Lee Universities' Pre-Health Club. The Pre-Health Club is an organization for students interested in a career in the health and medical field. The club serves as a tremendous resource to pre-med students. It connects them with alumni and other medical programs which allows them to continue their medical education beyond W&L.

Our group research our client's audience and conducted interviews with the Pre-Health Club leadership. We then took our research and coded, styled, and designed a website to showcase a specific event hosted by the Pre-Health Club: The Annual Ethics in Healthcare Panel.
